I'm a news junkie.

My name is Jenna Johnson and I'm a multi-media journalist, news junkie, writer, and world traveller. I love telling stories, and making them matter. There is a historical and cultural context to every story, and I constantly draw on my global background to analyze the bigger picture.

I believe that the journalism industry is everchanging, and it takes innovation to keep audience attention and stay a step ahead of the competition. This aspect of news fills me with adrenaline and joy, and has sparked my interest in news production.

When I don't have my nose stuck in news, I am also a human. In my free time, I enjoy yoga, reading, poetry, and making bad puns. (Sorry in advance).
