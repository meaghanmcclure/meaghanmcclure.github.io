Welcome to my site!

My name is Meaghan McClure and I'm a junior at the University of Miami. As you can tell by my double major in broadcast journalism and sports administration, I am passionate about sports, the media, and the way each of these influence society.

How fans and the public react to sports is unique to the industry, and the arena of sports media is ever-growing and constantly changing. For all these reasons, my interest has been sparked in the area, with a possible focus on sports media relations.

This site should work as a guide to both my professional and personal life; view my previous sample works, take a peak at my resume, or feel free to get in touch with me through the "Contact" page. Thanks for visiting!
